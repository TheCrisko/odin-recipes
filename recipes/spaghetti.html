Spaghetti

An easy, delicious, affordable weeknight dish anyone can cook!

Spaghetti and meatballs has been a recipe past down from generation to generation. Something anyone can enjoy whether it be from the long of spaghetti covered in delicious red pasta sauce or the simple homemade meatballs. This recipe will give you another dish to add to your weeknight rotation.

Cooking time: 20-30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1lb Spaghetti Pasta
  • 24oz Tomato Sauce
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Italian Breadcrumbs/Panko
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 Cajun Seasoning
  1. Preheat Oven to 400â„‰
  2. In a mixing bowl, add egg, breadcrumbs, parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Add ground beef, break apart and form balls with hands or icecream scooper
  4. Place finished balls on baking sheet lined with aluminum foil. Place in oven
  5. Bring water in a pot to boil over medium-high heat and add spaghetti once at a roaring boil
  6. Cook pasta to al dente and strain. Place back in pot and add tomato sauce along with the meatballs
  7. Serve and enjoy!
