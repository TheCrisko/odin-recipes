Bacon Cheeseburgers

A classic diner dish made homemade is even better!

Bacon and Hamburgers, two things Americans can't get enough of coming together to create a simple and delicious weeknight or weekend dish.

Cooking time: 15-20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 8oz hamburger patty
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp seasoning salt
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 Brioche Bun

Steps:

  1. On a skillet, preferably cast iron, heat over medium-high
  2. Butter bottoms of buns and add to skillet to toast
  3. Remove buns once toasted and then add bacon strips
  4. Cook bacon until done and then remove. Add hamurger patty
  5. Cook for about 5 minutes and then flip.
  6. After 2-3 minutes add cheese slices and cover skillet with pan to trap in steam/heat to melt cheese
  7. Once cheese is melted remove from skillet and assemble burger
  8. Enjoy!
